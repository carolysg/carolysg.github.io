About me

Hi! My name is Carolina, but you can call me Carol or Yumi. I'm 30 years old and I live in São Carlos, SP.

I have a bachelor's degree in civil engineering from the Federal University of São Carlos (UFSCar) and a master's degree in transportation engineering from the School of Engineering of São Carlos at the University of São Paulo (EESC-USP).

I work as a data engineer and I'm also pursuing a bachelor's degree in data science at the Virtual University of the State of São Paulo (UNIVESP).

I've loved swimming since I was a child, and I enjoy training and competing. In my free time, I like to watch animes and read detective novels.
